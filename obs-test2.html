How to Find Your Player ID

▶
  1. Open the Season 10 Leaderboard
  2. Click your player name
  3. Copy the full URL of your player page (or the numbers following player/ in the URL)
  4. Paste above!

How to Add Overlays to OBS

▶
  1. Copy the full URL of your overlay
  2. In OBS: Sources → + → Browser
  3. Paste the URL and remove default CSS

Recommended Dimensions

▶
  • Simple Elo: 600×240
  • Last Match: 480×400
  • Rank Position: 450×600
  • Leaderboard: 450×650

Troubleshooting

▶
  • If overlays seem laggy or broken, disable Browser Source Hardware Acceleration in OBS Settings
  • Restart OBS afterward
