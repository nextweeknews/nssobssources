How to Find Your Player ID

▶
  1. Open the Season 10 Leaderboard
  2. Click your player name
  3. Copy the full URL of your player page (or the numbers following player/ in the URL)
  4. Paste above!

How to Add Overlays to OBS

▶
  1. In an overlay page, copy the full URL
  2. Open OBS, then go to Sources > + > Browser
  3. Paste full URL and set the recommended dimensions

Recommended Dimensions

▶
  • Simple Elo: 600×240
  • Last Match: 480×400
  • Rank Position: 450×600
  • Leaderboard: 450×650

Troubleshooting

▶
  • If overlays are lagging, freezing, or seem broken, you may need to disable Browser Source Hardware Acceleration in OBS
  • Apply this in Settings/Preferences > Advanced > Sources and then restart OBS
