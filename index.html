How to Find Your Player ID

▶
  1. Open the Season 10 Leaderboard
  2. Find and click your player name
  3. In the URL, copy the string of numbers after /player/...
  4. Paste it into the search box above!

How to Add Overlays to OBS

▶
  1. Open your overlay & copy its full URL
  2. In OBS, go to Sources → + → Browser
  3. Select “Create New”
  4. Paste URL, set dimensions, and remove any default CSS

Recommended Dimensions

▶
  • Simple Elo: 600×240
  • Last Match: 480×400
  • Rank Position: 450×600
  • Leaderboard: 500×650
  • This is just for the setup. You can scale them as desired in your scene!

Troubleshooting

▶
  • If your browser sources seem laggy, frozen, or broken, you may need to disable Browser Source Hardware Acceleration.
  • Do this in OBS Settings/Preferences → Advanced → Sources, and restart OBS.
